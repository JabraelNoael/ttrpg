# Terminology/Goal

1. Whenever I say <span style="color: #3b7bf4;">\t</span> I literally mean imagine hitting tab at this point
2. Treat all `\n` as literal new-lines (push me back on if my formatting is no possible w/ the autofill set up)
3. Any new-line that begins with <span style="color: #4de156;">></span> is how I expect the terminal to respond
4. INSTRUCTIONS.md is nice and all but ideally you can navigate EVERYTHING just by experimenting with typing random stuff and hitting tab to reveal more possibilities iteratively


# Usage

/character <span style="color: #3b7bf4;">\t</span>
<span style="color: #4de156;">></span> <add|modify|remove>
